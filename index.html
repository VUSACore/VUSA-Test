Practice — A note on what we do, and what we refuse to do.
The Practice

A consultancy that operates, not one that observes.

VUSA Consultancy is a UK-registered commercial consultancy specialising in international commodity trade and cross-border supply chains. We are execution-led. Our clients are mid-market importers, exporters, and trading companies across North America and Europe.

Most consultancies sell frameworks. We sell movement. The contracts that close. The shipments that clear. The supplier relationships that hold under pressure. The documentation that survives a bank's compliance desk on a Friday afternoon.

We do not provide regulated financial or investment advice. We support the commercial and documentation discipline that real trades require — and we hold ourselves to the standard of being useful by Tuesday morning, not impressive in a deck.

The Approach

Three disciplines, uncompromised.

"The deals that close are not the deals that were pitched best. They are the deals that were prepared best, drafted tightest, and chased hardest."

— I.

Embedded, not advisory

We sit inside client operations rather than presenting from outside them. Daily standups, shared workflows, and a seat in the procurement and compliance reviews that matter. The output is movement, not slides.

— II.

Documentation discipline

Sales and Purchase Agreements drafted to survive bank scrutiny. Letters of Intent, ICPOs, FCOs, and Incoterms applied correctly the first time. We treat every clause as the one that will eventually be read by a lawyer.

— III.

Selective engagement

We work with a deliberately small number of clients each quarter. The economics of execution-led work mean we cannot be everywhere. We choose the engagements where our discipline genuinely changes the outcome.

The Engagement

From brief to delivery.

A four-stage process that holds across every engagement, regardless of sector. Disciplined at the front so the back is clean.

i

Brief

Direct conversation with the principal. Scope, sector, transaction profile, and counterparty exposure assessed inside the first call.

ii

Structure

Engagement scope, commercial structure, and documentation roadmap agreed in writing. No work begins on a handshake.

iii

Embed

Integration with client procurement, finance, and compliance functions. Shared workflows, weekly delivery cadence.

iv

Execute

Contracts drafted, supplier and buyer relationships managed, shipments coordinated, settlement closed. Delivery, not deliverables.
